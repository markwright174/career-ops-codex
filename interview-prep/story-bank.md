# Story Bank — Master STAR+R Stories

This file accumulates your best interview stories over time. Each evaluation (Block F) adds new stories here. Instead of memorizing 100 answers, maintain 5-10 deep stories that you can bend to answer almost any behavioral question.

## How it works

1. Every time `/career-ops oferta` generates Block F (Interview Plan), new STAR+R stories get appended here
2. Before your next interview, review this file — your stories are already organized by theme
3. The "Big Three" questions can be answered with stories from this bank:
   - "Tell me about yourself" → combine 2-3 stories into a narrative
   - "Tell me about your most impactful project" → pick your highest-impact story
   - "Tell me about a conflict you resolved" → find a story with a Reflection

### [Learning Architecture] Proficiency-Based Education Transition
**Source:** Report #001 - SWCA - Talent Development Director
**S (Situation):** Texas State Technical College needed to transition programs toward Proficiency-Based Education delivery.
**T (Task):** Help shape the learning design approach so the transition was structured, teachable, and aligned to competencies.
**A (Action):** Led project management, supported curriculum deconstruction, aligned competencies and learning objectives, and coordinated stakeholders around the new delivery model.
**R (Result):** Created a concrete example of curriculum architecture tied to organizational change rather than one-off course production.
**Reflection:** This story works best when I show that curriculum design is not just content work - it is systems work that supports adoption and behavior change.
**Best for questions about:** curriculum design, change management, learning architecture, stakeholder alignment
